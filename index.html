About

For the last 7 years Voorhees artist, Suzy Sherbine, has been creating sculptures made out of materials she finds on the ground in Camden or side of the road. Inspired by a course on Education for Sustainability by the Cloud Institute of NY and the film Poet of Poverty, which dealt with the decline and degradation of Camden, by the Center for Environmental Transformation, Suzy has made it her mission to speak out about the social and environmental injustices she sees through her art. By weaving some of the history of Camden into her pieces, America’s poorest city becomes the lens through which we see and examine our region’s most pressing environmental issues. Using imagination with a sense of humor Suzy has created the Exhibit ArtofFACTS Trash Transformed to Teach. Suzy is a member of Sustainable Cherry’s Art Team and a member of the Voorhees Art Council.
